About Rudra International

Your Trusted Agro Sourcing Partner

Founded in 2018 by Hiren Kanabar, Rudra International specializes in sourcing and exporting premium agro commodities from India to global markets. We bridge the gap between Indian farmers and international buyers with full transparency.

🔍

Personal Inspection

Every shipment is personally inspected by our team to ensure it meets international quality standards before dispatch.

📊

Market Intelligence

🤝

End-to-End Support

From sourcing and grading to packing and logistics — we coordinate every step through production and shipping.

How We Work

Our Export Process

A transparent, quality-focused process from your inquiry to doorstep delivery.

01

Inquiry & Requirements

Share your product requirements, quantity, destination, and preferred quality grade with us.

02

Sourcing & Sampling

We source from verified suppliers across India and send samples for your approval before proceeding.

03

Processing & Quality Check

Products go through cleaning, de-coating, grading, and packing with personal inspection at every stage.

04

Shipment & Delivery

We handle documentation, customs coordination, and real-time shipment tracking until delivery.
